Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Salted Butter
  • 0.5 TSP Salt
  • 1/2 Cup White Sugar
  • 1 Cup Dark Brown Sugar
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 TSP Vanilla Extract
  • 2.5 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 TSP Baking Soda
  • 2 Cups Semisweet Chocolate Chips
  • 1 Liter of the Blood of Your Enemies

Description

This is a recipe for some delicious chocolate chip cookies.

 More About Chocolate Chip Cookies

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F.
  2. In medium bowl combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Mix well with wire whisk.
  4. Set aside.
  5. Add butter and mix to form a grainy paste, scraping down the sides of the bowl.
  6. Pour in all of your enemies' blood.
  7. Add the flour mixture and chocolate chips, and blend at low speed until just mixed.
  8. Drop by rounded TBSPs onto an ungreased cookie sheet, 2 inches apart.
  9. Bake 22-24 minutes or until golden brown.
  10. Transfer cookies immediately to a cool surface with a spatula.
  11. Devour ravenously and absorb your enemies' powers.
